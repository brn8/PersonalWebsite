I arrived in the United States in 2010, fulfilling a dream shared by many who view this country as the land of opportunity. My journey began when I started 7th grade here, which came with its own set of challenges. Learning English was initially difficult, and adjusting to a new culture and education system was intimidating. However, as time passed, I adapted, improving my language skills and embracing my new environment.

I graduated from William L. Dickinson High School, an experience that strengthened my academic foundation and helped me develop a passion for technology. This led me to pursue a degree in computer science. I began my college journey at Montclair State University, where I spent two years honing my technical skills and building a strong academic background. Later, I transferred to the New Jersey Institute of Technology (NJIT), where I completed my degree in 2021. My time at NJIT was transformative, allowing me to dive deeper into computer science, explore specialized areas, and prepare myself for a career in technology.

Beyond academics, I have a deep love for sports, which has always been a big part of my life. I enjoy both watching and playing sports, especially basketball, cricket, baseball, football, and carrom. Sports have taught me invaluable lessons in teamwork, discipline, and perseverance, which I carry with me in all aspects of my life.
